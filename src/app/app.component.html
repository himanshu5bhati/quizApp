Welcome to the Quiz

Please adhere to these instructions at all time:

  • You will be presented a question with 4 option on your screen. you have to choose one.
  • In case of multiselect more than one option will comprise as correct answer.
  • Each question has a set timelimit which will appear on top right. once timer is up you cannot go back to question.
  • Once you click next that question will be locked you can't go back to the question to make changes.
  • Once you submit the test you will be shown your answers for each question and whether they are correct or not
  • You can restart the test by clicking on restart button.
